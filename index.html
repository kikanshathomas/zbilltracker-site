FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is my financial data private?

Yes — everything stays on your device. There's no account, no cloud, and no tracking, and the app works fully offline. You can export a backup file any time to keep or move your data.

What happens if I miss a bill?

The survivor tied to that bill gets hurt, then infected — keep ignoring it and they turn. Paying the bill saves them. It's a deadline you feel, but your real records and amounts are never affected.

Can I turn the zombies off?

Yes. Plain mode switches the whole app to a straight bill ledger, and a light theme is available there too.

Is it really pay-once?

Yes — $3.99 one time on Google Play. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no ads.

Does it work offline?

100%. The app never connects to a server and no account is needed — reminders, loans, insights, and the story log all run entirely on your device.

Which languages are supported?

English, French, Italian, German, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese — eight in total, switchable in the app.
